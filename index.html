What is Campus Crush?

Campus Crush is a dating app designed for Nigerian university students. #loveisinsession is more than a slogan -- it’s our commitment to bringing you closer to finding your crush with safe, fun, and personalised connections.

Customizable Profiles

Personalize your profile to reflect your personality and interests. Showcase what makes you unique to find like-minded individuals or explore new connections.

Verified Connections

Our advanced verification system ensures that every user is genuine and belongs to a Nigerian university, creating a safe and trustworthy environment for everyone.

Plan Hangouts

Looking for the perfect date spot? Discover curated hangout spots and cozy cafés near your campus to meet your crush.

Dual Dating Feature

Explore relationships within your university or expand your network to connect with students from other campuses across Nigeria.

Early Party Access

Get notified about exclusive campus parties and events happening near you. Connect in real life and make unforgettable memories with your matches.

Ice Breaking Games

Break the ice in a fun and creative way. Play games like “Would You Rather?” and “Trivia” within the chat to spark exciting conversations.
